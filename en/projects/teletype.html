Brief and problem statement

The client came with a desire to refresh the brand style.

We sat down with the brief and thought it through: what the product is, who it's for, what its strengths and weaknesses are.

Functionally the service was strong, but its communication lacked clarity and cohesion.

We framed the challenge as follows — in plain, clear language position Teletype as a cloud service for business customer communication, and establish clear differentiation from competitors.
