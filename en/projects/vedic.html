2

Not fortune-telling — education

2025: Faladdin's founder was arrested. "Fal" (fortune-telling) is a legal risk in Turkey. Positioning: education and self-awareness, not predictions. "Shows a tendency" instead of "this will happen." Classical texts (BPHS, Phaladeepika, Brihat Jataka) as the source. The platform explains concepts — Lagna, Rashi, Nakshatras, Dasha — in Turkish.

Six products: calculator, Sade Sati report, Dasha Timing, glossary, consulting, forum
3

Birth chart calculator

Step-by-step form: date, time, city. VedAstro for calculations, OpenStreetMap for geocoding, TimeAPI for time zones. Real astronomical calculations, not generative AI. Free tier: basic chart. Paid reports: detailed AI interpretation via Claude. The calculator is embedded right in the landing — try it without navigating away.

Calculator: step-by-step form, embedded in landing
4

Sade Sati — flagship product

Saturn's 7.5-year transit through three phases. For many in Turkey — their first encounter with Vedic astrology. Personalized report: timeline, current phase, recommendations.

Content strategy: Sade Sati as the entry point. Educational page explains the concept, calculator shows personal dates, paid report provides deep analysis.

Sade Sati: educational content, three transit phases, personal timeline

Content platform

Glossary of 50+ Vedic terms in Turkish. Each term has a page with the Sanskrit original, explanation, and context. Foundation for SEO: zero competition for Turkish Vedic queries. Forum for discussions, blog for educational articles, "Weekly Stellar Insights" newsletter. Everything bilingual: Turkish + English through an i18n system.

Paid reports: Sade Sati, birth chart, bundle. 799–1,499 TRY via Shopier with taksit (installments — a cultural norm in Turkey).

Future: "VedikCircle" subscription, courses, personal consultations.
